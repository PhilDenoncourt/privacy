Privacy Policy for Mini Golf Scorecard

Last updated: July 8, 2025

Introduction

Mini Golf Scorecard ("we," "our," or "us") operates the Mini Golf Scorecard mobile application (the "Service").

This page informs you of our policies regarding the collection, use, and disclosure of personal data when you use our Service.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • Player Names: Names you enter for scorecard players are stored locally on your device
  • Game Data: Scorecards, scores, and game statistics are stored locally on your device

Location Information

  • Optional Location Data: If you choose to enable location services, we may collect and store your approximate location when starting a game
  • Purpose: Location data is used solely to record where you played mini golf courses for your personal reference
  • Control: You can disable location access at any time through your device settings

Photos

  • Game Photos: Photos you take or select during games are stored locally on your device
  • Camera/Gallery Access: We request camera and photo library permissions only when you choose to add photos to your scorecards

How We Use Your Information

  • All data is stored locally on your device
  • No personal information is transmitted to external servers
  • Data is used solely for app functionality (scorekeeping, statistics, photo storage)

Data Storage and Security

  • All app data is stored locally on your device using secure storage methods
  • No data is transmitted to external servers or third parties
  • You can export your data or delete all data through the app settings

Children's Privacy

Our Service does not knowingly collect personally identifiable information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Third-Party Services

This app does not integrate with any third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data (view in app)
  • Export your data (backup feature)
  • Delete your data (clear all data feature)
  • Control location permissions through device settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the "Last updated" date of this Privacy Policy.
